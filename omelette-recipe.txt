Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/4 cup milk
2 teaspoons fish sauce
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons oil

Directions:

1. Place frying pan on stove and set heat to MAX. Add 2 teaspoons oil

2. Break eggs and mix contents in a bowl. Add in the other ingredients
and continue mixing.

3. Pour mixture into pan and move cooked parts over to the side with
a spatula

4. When little mixture is left, fold omelette in half and flip. Turn off heat
